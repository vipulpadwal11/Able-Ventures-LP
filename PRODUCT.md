# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
* **Founders**: Looking for a specialized venture studio partner in fintech/healthtech/age-tech who brings team-building, validation, and operational expertise rather than just passive capital.
* **Operators**: Talented builders (engineers, PMs, designers) looking to join next-generation companies in the healthy aging ecosystem.
* **Investors & LPs**: Partners looking for a disciplined, repeatable venture-building model with a strong regional and global thesis.
* **Strategic Partners**: Healthcare systems, financial institutions, and industry partners seeking collaboration in healthy aging and quality-of-life tech.

## Product Purpose
The website serves as the digital headquarters of Able Ventures. It must communicate what the studio does, why it exists, how the co-creation model works, and drive high-quality inbound inquiries from founders, operators, and strategic partners.

## Brand Personality
* **Visionary**: Focused on long-term demographic transformations and optimistic opportunities in healthy aging.
* **Credible**: Professional, builder-led, and backed by deep execution and domain expertise.
* **Human & Sophisticated**: Centered on improving actual lives while looking premium, minimalist, and expensive.

## Anti-references
* **Generic VC templates**: Overused neon gradients, dark futuristic grids, or hyper-promotional tech marketing copy.
* **Corporate consultancies**: Stiff stock photos of handshakes, abstract blue waves, or generic corporate jargon.
* **AI Monoculture**: Warm "cream/sand/beige/paper" backgrounds paired with wide-spaced uppercase eyebrows on every single section.

## Design Principles
1. **Purpose-Driven Layout**: Every section must explicitly answer a business question (e.g., "Why aging matters", "How the studio model works").
2. **Restrained Elegance**: Use our logo-derived warm off-white, vibrant coral, and deep slate palette with generous whitespace to create a bespoke, premium editorial feel.
3. **Show, Don't Tell**: Explain the venture studio model using clean, interactive step-by-step visual flows instead of dense paragraphs.
4. **Context-Rich Placeholders**: Fully realize fintech/healthtech concepts and sample articles rather than relying on lazy lorem ipsum or generic stock imagery.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* Target compliance: WCAG 2.2 AA.
* Proper color contrast ratio (≥ 4.5:1 for body copy).
* Keyboard navigation for interactive widgets and timelines.
* Absolute support for `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)`.
