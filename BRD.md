# Business Requirements Document

## Able Ventures Website Redesign

**Version:** 1.0
**Document Type:** Business Requirements Document (BRD)
**Project:** Able Ventures Website Redesign
**Prepared By:** Independent Product & Design Proposal
**Date:** June 2026

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Able Ventures is positioned as a venture studio and investment platform focused on building and backing companies that improve quality of life, particularly within fintech, healthtech, age-tech, and adjacent sectors. While the company's mission is compelling, its current digital presence does not adequately communicate the scale of its ambition, investment philosophy, venture-building capabilities, or long-term vision.

The purpose of this project is to design and develop a modern venture studio website that clearly communicates what Able Ventures does, who it serves, what it believes, and how it creates value. The website should function as a trust-building asset for founders, investors, partners, operators, and prospective portfolio companies.

Rather than acting as a simple company brochure, the new website should become a strategic business asset that strengthens credibility, attracts high-quality founders, showcases expertise, and positions Able Ventures as a category-defining venture studio focused on the future of healthy and financially secure aging.

The website must communicate sophistication without complexity. Visitors should understand the firm's mission, focus areas, operating model, and value proposition within the first few seconds of arriving on the homepage.

---

# 2. Business Context

The global population is aging rapidly. At the same time, healthcare systems, financial infrastructure, housing models, and care delivery systems remain fragmented and outdated. This creates a significant opportunity for companies that can improve the quality of life, independence, financial security, and overall wellbeing of aging populations.

Able Ventures exists at the intersection of venture creation and capital allocation. Unlike traditional venture capital firms that primarily invest in external startups, venture studios play a more active role in identifying opportunities, validating ideas, assembling teams, and helping build companies from the ground up.

This distinction is strategically important but often misunderstood. Therefore, the website must educate visitors on what makes Able Ventures different while simultaneously showcasing the outcomes of that model.

The redesigned website should serve as the public expression of the company's vision, operating philosophy, and long-term commitment to building transformational businesses.

---

# 3. Business Objectives

The primary objective of the website is to establish credibility and trust among all stakeholders interacting with Able Ventures.

The website should communicate that Able Ventures is not merely investing in startups, but actively participating in the creation of businesses capable of generating meaningful impact.

The website should position Able Ventures as a long-term builder rather than a passive investor.

The website should attract:

* Exceptional founders
* Startup operators
* Potential investment partners
* Strategic collaborators
* Talent interested in joining portfolio companies
* Industry stakeholders interested in aging-related innovation

The website should clearly communicate the company's investment thesis and venture-building philosophy while making it easy for interested parties to initiate contact.

---

# 4. Strategic Positioning

The website should position Able Ventures as:

### A Venture Studio

A firm that identifies opportunities, validates ideas, builds teams, and launches companies.

### A Long-Term Partner

A company focused on creating sustainable businesses rather than pursuing short-term outcomes.

### A Sector Specialist

An organization deeply focused on challenges and opportunities related to aging populations.

### A Builder-Led Organization

A team that actively participates in company creation rather than merely providing capital.

### A Global Player

A Dubai-based company with ambitions and opportunities that extend beyond regional boundaries.

---

# 5. Target Audiences

## Founders

Founders are one of the most important audiences for the website.

They need confidence that Able Ventures understands the industries in which they operate and can contribute more than capital alone.

Founders should leave the website believing that Able Ventures can help accelerate company creation, reduce execution risk, and provide strategic guidance.

---

## Startup Operators

Product managers, engineers, designers, growth specialists, and operators represent a critical audience.

These individuals may become future founding team members or key hires within portfolio companies.

The website should communicate that Able Ventures values execution, ownership, and long-term thinking.

---

## Investors and LPs

Potential investors need confidence that Able Ventures possesses a clear investment thesis, strong domain expertise, and a repeatable venture-building model.

The website should communicate discipline, professionalism, and strategic clarity.

---

## Strategic Partners

Healthcare providers, financial institutions, research organizations, government bodies, and industry partners need to understand where collaboration opportunities exist.

The website should establish trust and demonstrate industry expertise.

---

## Media and Industry Stakeholders

Journalists, conference organizers, and industry researchers may use the website as a source of information about the company.

The website should provide a clear narrative and easily accessible company information.

---

# 6. Business Challenges

The current digital presence creates several challenges.

The company's value proposition is not immediately obvious.

Visitors may struggle to understand:

* What Able Ventures actually does
* Whether it is an investment firm, startup studio, or consultancy
* Which industries it focuses on
* What differentiates it from traditional venture capital firms

This ambiguity creates friction and reduces trust.

A modern website must remove this confusion and establish clarity immediately.

---

# 7. Key Messages

Every page should reinforce the following messages:

Able Ventures exists to improve quality of life through technology.

Able Ventures focuses on sectors that will define the future of aging.

Able Ventures actively builds and supports companies.

Able Ventures combines operational expertise with investment capital.

Able Ventures partners with exceptional founders and operators.

Able Ventures is building for decades, not quarters.

---

# 8. Success Metrics

The redesigned website will be considered successful if it improves both qualitative and quantitative outcomes.

Quantitatively, success can be measured through:

* Increased website traffic
* Increased direct inquiries
* Increased founder applications
* Increased partnership requests
* Increased time on site
* Reduced bounce rate
* Growth in newsletter signups
* Growth in inbound opportunities

Qualitatively, success can be measured through:

* Improved brand perception
* Clear understanding of the venture studio model
* Stronger positioning within fintech, healthtech, and age-tech sectors
* Increased credibility among founders and investors

---

# 9. Brand Personality

The website should feel:

### Visionary

Focused on long-term societal transformation.

### Credible

Grounded in expertise and execution.

### Human

Centered around improving lives.

### Sophisticated

Modern, premium, and professional.

### Optimistic

Focused on opportunity and innovation.

The website should avoid startup clichés, excessive jargon, and generic venture capital language.

---

# 10. Scope

The first release should include:

* Homepage
* About
* Focus Areas
* Portfolio
* Venture Studio Model
* Insights
* Contact

The website should be designed to accommodate future expansion including:

* Portfolio company profiles
* Founder resources
* Careers
* Events
* Research reports
* Investment updates

---

# 11. Risks

Because limited public information is available regarding Able Ventures' portfolio and operating model, assumptions have been made based on publicly available positioning.

The website should therefore be designed in a flexible manner that allows content to be updated as additional company information becomes available.

---

# 12. Conclusion

The new Able Ventures website should function as more than a marketing asset. It should become the digital headquarters of the firm's vision.

By clearly communicating the firm's mission, venture-building philosophy, focus areas, and long-term ambition, the website can establish Able Ventures as a trusted builder of transformative companies focused on improving how people age, live, and thrive.

The end result should position Able Ventures as one of the most compelling venture studio brands operating within the future-of-aging ecosystem.
