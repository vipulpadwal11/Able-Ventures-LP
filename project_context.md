# Important Constraints

The public information available about Able Ventures is limited.

Their current website is unavailable and returns a 403 Forbidden error.

Therefore:

* Do not invent portfolio companies.
* Do not invent team members.
* Do not invent investment metrics.
* Do not invent funding numbers.
* Do not invent partnerships.

Instead, create a premium venture studio experience based on their publicly visible positioning and mission.

The website should feel realistic, credible, and professional.

---

# Project Goal

The website should communicate:

1. What Able Ventures does.
2. Why it exists.
3. What sectors it focuses on.
4. How its venture studio model works.
5. Why founders, operators, and partners should engage with them.

A visitor should understand the company's purpose within the first few seconds of arriving.

---

# Website Structure

The website consists of:

* Home

* Focus Areas
* Portfolio

* Contact

The homepage should act as the primary storytelling experience.

# Design Direction

Avoid:

* Generic VC websites
* Corporate consulting websites
* SaaS landing page clichés
* Overused startup illustrations
* Excessive gradients
* Excessive animations

The visual direction should feel closer to:

* Modern venture studios
* Premium investment firms
* High-end technology companies
* Future-focused organizations

Keywords:

* Premium
* Minimal
* Editorial
* Sophisticated
* Visionary
* Human
* Trustworthy

The website should feel expensive and intentional.

Every section must have a purpose.
